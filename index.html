In To The World Of Harry Potter

The Harry Potter series follows the life and adventures of Harry Potter, an orphaned boy who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he meets new friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. Throughout the series, Harry learns about his magical heritage, confronts dark forces, and ultimately faces the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who seeks to conquer the wizarding world.

J.K Rowling

Joanne Rowling (born 31 July 1965), known by her pen name J. K. Rowling, is a British author and philanthropist. She wrote Harry Potter, a seven-volume fantasy series published from 1997 to 2007. The series has sold over 600 million copies, been translated into 84 languages, and spawned a global media franchise including films and video games. The Casual Vacancy (2012) was her first novel for adults. She writes Cormoran Strike, an ongoing crime fiction series, under the alias Robert Galbraith.
